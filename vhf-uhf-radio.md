## VHF/UHF Radio

<div class="station-card">
  <h3>Overview</h3>
  <p>
    This page collects some of the most popular Seattle-area voice repeaters that are useful for day-to-day
    listening and local operating. The focus here is practical analog voice coverage in and around Seattle,
    Shoreline, and the broader Puget Sound metro area.
  </p>
  <p>
    Repeaters and usage patterns can change over time, but the list below is a solid working starting point
    for local monitoring and programming.
  </p>
</div>
