What MUF Means

MUF stands for Maximum Usable Frequency. It is the highest frequency likely to reflect off the ionosphere over a given path.

Higher MUF usually means upper HF bands like 10m and 15m may open up. Lower MUF usually means 20m, 40m, and 80m will be more dependable.
