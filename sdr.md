<div class="station-card">
  <h3>What You Can Listen For</h3>
  <ul class="station-list">
    <li><span class="station-label">HF:</span> Amateur radio voice, FT8, WSPR, and band activity across the configured HF profiles.</li>
    <li><span class="station-label">VHF / UHF:</span> Local simplex, repeaters, NOAA weather radio, marine channels, and regional utility activity depending on the selected profile.</li>
    <li><span class="station-label">Airband:</span> Sea-Tac area aviation frequencies including ATIS, tower, ground, and approach.</li>
    <li><span class="station-label">Mesh / ISM:</span> Meshtastic and Meshcore activity can be seen as bursts in the 900 MHz ISM range.</li>
  </ul>
</div>

<div class="station-card">
  <h3>Quick Tips</h3>
  <ul class="station-list">
    <li><span class="station-label">Bookmarks:</span> Use the bookmarks dropdown to jump to common HF, VHF, UHF, marine, NOAA, airband, and mesh frequencies.</li>
    <li><span class="station-label">Profiles:</span> Switch receiver profiles to move between HF, airband, 2m, 70cm, and 900 MHz mesh coverage.</li>
    <li><span class="station-label">Modes:</span> AM is used for airband, USB/LSB for HF voice and digital areas, and NFM for most VHF/UHF channels.</li>
    <li><span class="station-label">Waterfall:</span> Mesh activity will appear as short bursts or chirps rather than decodable audio in OpenWebRX.</li>
  </ul>
</div>
